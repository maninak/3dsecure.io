How 3-D Secure helps your bussiness

Preempts and drastically reduces fraudulent chargebacks
Increases online purchasing due to boosted cardholder confidence
Reduces declined transactions and processing delays
Shifts transaction liability from merchant bank to card issuing bank

At it's core, the 3-D Secure protocol is a 2-Factor authentication system adapted for credit card transactions.

It's a security solution verifying that the transacting customer is the legitimate owner of the credit card used, by requiring an additional password.That password is only shared between the legitimate card holder and the bank that issued the card. Often in real-time via SMS.

3-D Decure systems, including Verified by Visa® and MasterCard SecureCode®, automatically forward most of the liability from the Payment Gateway and the Merchant, back to the card-issuing bank instead.
